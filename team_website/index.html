About Evalyo

The Problem

Students at Hunter College use the site RateMyProfessors to evaluate and choose their professors. RateMyProfessors is widely used application across campuses in the United States, where students can review former professors. Students both generate and recieve the site's content.

However, RateMyProfessors has loose community standards which often leads to site abuse by disgruntled students. The site is filled with devastating reviews, and in return professors avoid the site.

Similarly, Hunter College does provide its own professor evaluation database to students. But still, the interface is not exactly user friendly, and we can only view one section at a time. Making it difficult to compare and contrast multiple section at once.

The Solution

Enter Evalyo! It will be a complete revamp of Hunter's professor evaluation database system with a modern twist. Furthermore, Evalyo will inorporate brand new features such as being able view your results based on class sections, courses, or professors. In return, it allows students to compare and contrast their results. User will also be able to see professors' overall ratings based on what previous students has given them. While it is all presented with a pleasant, easy-to-navigate interface created with modern tools.

In return, you can evaluate without the wait.
